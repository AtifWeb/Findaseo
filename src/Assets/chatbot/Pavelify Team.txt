

Hey there, welcome to Pavelify. 
Here are a few things to get you started. We hope you enjoy Pavelify and we look forward to supporting you should you have any questions or need any assistance. 

Install the live chat widget to chat with your site visitors (https://app.pavelify.com/settings/LiveChat )
Upload a profile photo and update your profile (https://app.pavelify.com/settings/Account)
Connect your support email to reply to customers  (https://app.pavelify.com/settings/EmailSetup)
Setup appointment scheduling (https://app.pavelify.com/CalendarBooking/calendars)

 Add team members /operators  (https://app.pavelify.com/operators) 

Integrate your apps (https://app.pavelify.com/settings/Integrations)
